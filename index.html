About Us

Our Brief History

We are a team of talanted and passionate photographers, graphic designers, makeup artists, and more. We combine the power of art and photography to create compelling images which will make you shine.

Pendrite Photography was formed in 2015 by our Founder and Chief Executive Officer Amanda Jepson with a belief that when passionate people work together and are empowered, amazing things can happen.

In these few years, Pendrite Photography has made its mark in Los Angeles as well as well as recognized internationally for excellent work and its impact on people, small and large businesses.

Together with Ronald Bridge our Cofounder and Marketing Lead, we have internationally maintained a small versatile team where our effort and commitment to each project is much larger. We believe that working closely with each other and in direct contacct with our clients will lead to better understanding of the project needs, and delivering better results.

Outside of our client work, we enjoy giving back to the community through the PG - Pendrite Give Back project, and helping aspiring photographers master their craft, and grow their businesses easily through our Pendrite Photography Institute
